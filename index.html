Interactive learning platform

SmartLearn Pro focuses on a user interactive system of learning to enhance user learning and information retainability. Studies show that humans get their information in the following way:

    More that 80% of the information is gotten by sight, 80% of which is remembered.
    11% of the information is obtained through hearing, 30% of which is remembered.
    3.5% of the information is obtained by smell.
    1.5% by touch and taste.
    50% of what what is both seen and heard is remembered.
    Further, 80% of what is seen, heard and done is remmbered.
SmartLearn Pro combines hearing and seeing through videos, seeing through the many books and doing through the interactive quizes, educational games. This improves information retaibility and concerntration comapred to traditional methods.
